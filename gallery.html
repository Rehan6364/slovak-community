Our Journey Through the Years

2010

Founding of the Charity

The Slovak Community Charity was established by a small group of Slovak expatriates in South Manchester.

2012

First Cultural Festival

We organized our first major cultural festival, attracting over 200 attendees from across the region.

2015

Official Charity Status

We registered as an official charity, allowing us to expand our programs and secure funding.

2017

Community Center Opening

We opened our dedicated community center, providing a permanent home for our activities.

2020

10th Anniversary

We celebrated our 10th anniversary with a virtual gala during the pandemic, connecting our community online.

2023

Expanding Our Reach

We continue to grow, with over 500 members and an expanding range of programs and services.
