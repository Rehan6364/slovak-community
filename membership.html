Membership Sign-Up

Join Our Community

Becoming a member of the Slovak Community Charity gives you access to all our events, workshops, and resources. Members receive discounted rates for paid events and regular updates about our activities.

Membership Benefits

  • Access to all community events and gatherings
  • Discounted rates for workshops and classes
  • Monthly newsletter with community updates
  • Voting rights at our annual general meeting
  • Opportunity to participate in organizing events
  • Access to our members-only online resources
