About Us

Our Mission

The Slovak Community Charity in South Manchester was established in 2010 with a clear mission: to preserve and celebrate Slovak culture, traditions, and language among the Slovak community in Manchester and surrounding areas.

We aim to create a welcoming environment where Slovaks and those interested in Slovak culture can connect, learn, and support each other while maintaining ties to our rich heritage.

Our History

What began as informal gatherings among a small group of Slovak expatriates has grown into a vibrant community organization serving hundreds of families across South Manchester.

Over the years, we have expanded our programs to include language classes, cultural workshops, traditional celebrations, and educational resources for children and adults alike.

In 2015, we officially registered as a charity, allowing us to better serve our community and secure funding for our various initiatives.

Our Impact

Through our programs and events, we have:

  • Helped over 500 Slovak families maintain connections to their cultural heritage
  • Taught Slovak language to more than 200 children born in the UK
  • Organized 50+ cultural events celebrating Slovak traditions
  • Created a support network for new arrivals from Slovakia
  • Built bridges between the Slovak community and the wider Manchester community
